About

I believe the best things take time – just like good compost, slow sunrises, and a well-seasoned cast-iron skillet. Tara, the quirky farm girl, started her all-organic farm in LaGrange, Georgia in 2019. The name is from “Haines Heaven” spelled backwards on a Star Wars-themed farm. We organically pasture-range Ayam Cemani, naturally raise honeybees, along with a few family pets — a dynamic duo of Leia and Han Solo the magnificent miniature donkeys — and a vast organic garden.

🍪 Cottage Food Baking

Small-batch, gluten-free and organic baked goods made with love and heritage recipes — like Farmhouse Chip Cookies and Organic Blackberry Fig and Lavender Jam, made from homegrown fruit and love.

🐝 Honey & Bees

Raw, golden honey harvested from our own apiaries. We believe in nurturing pollinators naturally and sharing our knowledge with aspiring beekeepers.

🌱 Organic Produce

Heirloom vegetables, garden-fresh herbs, and seasonal delights — all grown using time-tested methods and harvested with care.

🎪 Farmers Market

You can find Tara at The Market At The Rail — located at 206 E. Depot Street in LaGrange, Georgia.
Dates and hours vary, so check back often or follow on social media for updates!
