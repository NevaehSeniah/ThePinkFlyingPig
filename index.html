🍪 Cottage Food Baking

Small-batch, gluten-free, and organic baked goods made with love and heritage recipes — like our Farmhouse Chip Cookies and Organic Blackberry Fig and Lavender Jam, made from homegrown fruit and heart.

🌿 From Seed to Plate

Everything grown, gathered, or baked is done with time-honored practices and the joy of organic living. From canning jams to pasture-raising Rooster Roulettes, we believe in doing things the slow, honest way.

Farm Offerings

At Nevaeh Seniah, we organically pasture-range rare Ayam Cemani chickens, naturally raise honeybees, and nurture an abundant organic garden. Our two miniature donkeys, Han and Leia Solo, help with morale (and mischief).
